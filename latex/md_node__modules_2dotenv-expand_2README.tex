Dotenv-\/expand adds variable expansion on top of \href{http://github.com/motdotla/dotenv}{\texttt{ dotenv}}. If you find yourself needing to expand environment variables already existing on your machine, then dotenv-\/expand is your tool.

\href{https://travis-ci.org/motdotla/dotenv-expand}{\texttt{ }} \href{https://www.npmjs.com/package/dotenv-expand}{\texttt{ }} \href{https://github.com/feross/standard}{\texttt{ }}\hypertarget{md_node__modules_2dotenv-expand_2README_autotoc_md9668}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Install}{Install}}\label{md_node__modules_2dotenv-expand_2README_autotoc_md9668}

\end{DoxyCode}
\hypertarget{md_node__modules_2dotenv-expand_2README_autotoc_md9669}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Usage}{Usage}}\label{md_node__modules_2dotenv-expand_2README_autotoc_md9669}
As early as possible in your application, require dotenv and dotenv-\/expand, and wrap dotenv-\/expand around dotenv.


\begin{DoxyCode}{0}
\DoxyCodeLine{var\ dotenv\ =\ require('dotenv')}
\DoxyCodeLine{var\ dotenvExpand\ =\ require('dotenv-\/expand')}
\DoxyCodeLine{}
\DoxyCodeLine{var\ myEnv\ =\ dotenv.config()}
\DoxyCodeLine{dotenvExpand(myEnv)}

\end{DoxyCode}
