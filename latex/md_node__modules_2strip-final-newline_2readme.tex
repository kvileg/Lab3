Strip the final \href{https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Newline}{\texttt{ newline character}} from a string/buffer \end{quote}
Can be useful when parsing the output of, for example, {\ttfamily Child\+Process\#exec\+File}, as \href{https://stackoverflow.com/questions/729692/why-should-text-files-end-with-a-newline}{\texttt{ binaries usually output a newline at the end}}.
