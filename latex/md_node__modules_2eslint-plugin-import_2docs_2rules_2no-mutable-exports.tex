Forbids the use of mutable exports with {\ttfamily var} or {\ttfamily let}.\hypertarget{md_node__modules_2eslint-plugin-import_2docs_2rules_2no-mutable-exports_autotoc_md10939}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Rule Details}{Rule Details}}\label{md_node__modules_2eslint-plugin-import_2docs_2rules_2no-mutable-exports_autotoc_md10939}
Valid\+:


\begin{DoxyCode}{0}
\DoxyCodeLine{export\ const\ count\ =\ 1}
\DoxyCodeLine{export\ function\ getCount()\ \{\}}
\DoxyCodeLine{export\ class\ Counter\ \{\}}

\end{DoxyCode}


...whereas here exports will be reported\+:


\begin{DoxyCode}{0}
\DoxyCodeLine{export\ let\ count\ =\ 2}
\DoxyCodeLine{export\ var\ count\ =\ 3}
\DoxyCodeLine{}
\DoxyCodeLine{let\ count\ =\ 4}
\DoxyCodeLine{export\ \{\ count\ \}\ //\ reported\ here}

\end{DoxyCode}
\hypertarget{md_node__modules_2eslint-plugin-import_2docs_2rules_2no-mutable-exports_autotoc_md10940}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Functions/\+Classes}{Functions/Classes}}\label{md_node__modules_2eslint-plugin-import_2docs_2rules_2no-mutable-exports_autotoc_md10940}
Note that exported function/class declaration identifiers may be reassigned, but are not flagged by this rule at this time. They may be in the future, if a reassignment is detected, i.\+e.


\begin{DoxyCode}{0}
\DoxyCodeLine{//\ possible\ future\ behavior!}
\DoxyCodeLine{export\ class\ Counter\ \{\}\ //\ reported\ here:\ exported\ class\ is\ reassigned\ on\ line\ [x].}
\DoxyCodeLine{Counter\ =\ KitchenSink\ //\ not\ reported\ here\ unless\ you\ enable\ no-\/class-\/assign}
\DoxyCodeLine{}
\DoxyCodeLine{//\ this\ pre-\/declaration\ reassignment\ is\ valid\ on\ account\ of\ function\ hoisting}
\DoxyCodeLine{getCount\ =\ function\ getDuke()\ \{\}\ //\ not\ reported\ here\ without\ no-\/func-\/assign}
\DoxyCodeLine{export\ function\ getCount()\ \{\}\ //\ reported\ here:\ exported\ function\ is\ reassigned\ on\ line\ [x].}

\end{DoxyCode}


To prevent general reassignment of these identifiers, exported or not, you may want to enable the following core ESLint rules\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item \href{https://eslint.org/docs/rules/no-func-assign}{\texttt{ no-\/func-\/assign}}
\item \href{https://eslint.org/docs/rules/no-class-assign}{\texttt{ no-\/class-\/assign}}
\end{DoxyItemize}\hypertarget{md_node__modules_2eslint-plugin-import_2docs_2rules_2no-mutable-exports_autotoc_md10941}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{When Not To Use It}{When Not To Use It}}\label{md_node__modules_2eslint-plugin-import_2docs_2rules_2no-mutable-exports_autotoc_md10941}
If your environment correctly implements mutable export bindings.
