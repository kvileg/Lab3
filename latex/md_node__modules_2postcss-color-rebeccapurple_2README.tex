In 2014, Rebecca Alison Meyer, the daughter of \href{https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eric_A._Meyer}{\texttt{ Eric A. Meyer}}, an American web design consultant best known for his advocacy work on behalf of CSS, passed away of a brain tumor at six years of age.

In her memory, the hex color {\ttfamily \#663399} is named {\ttfamily rebeccapurple} and \href{https://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/www-style/2014Jun/0312.html}{\texttt{ added to the CSS Colors list}}.\hypertarget{md_node__modules_2postcss-color-rebeccapurple_2README_autotoc_md20775}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Usage}{Usage}}\label{md_node__modules_2postcss-color-rebeccapurple_2README_autotoc_md20775}
